# Process Improvement Assessment

This assessment identifies the key operational challenges observed during the discovery session and recommends practical improvements to standardize operations, improve visibility, reduce manual effort, and enhance the client experience.

The recommendations below are based solely on the information gathered during the initial stakeholder interview. Any assumptions made have been clearly identified.

---

# 1. Operational Challenges

| Challenge | Current Issue | Business Impact |
|------------|---------------|-----------------|
| Inconsistent Lead Management | Some referral leads are not recorded in HubSpot. | Loss of lead visibility and inaccurate sales pipeline. |
| Different Proposal Formats | Consultants prepare proposals differently. | Inconsistent client experience and additional review effort. |
| Incomplete Project Setup | Consultants sometimes begin work before projects are fully configured. | Missing documentation and poor project tracking. |
| Manual Resource Planning | Consultant capacity is tracked using spreadsheets. | Risk of overloading consultants and inefficient resource allocation. |
| Inconsistent Project Updates | Some consultants update Asana, while others use Slack or Email. | Difficult to monitor project progress and maintain transparency. |
| Scattered Documentation | Files are stored across Asana, Google Drive, and Email. | Increased time spent searching for information and higher risk of missing documents. |
| Delayed Billing | Consultants occasionally submit time entries late. | Delayed invoicing and slower revenue collection. |
| Lack of Standardized Delivery Process | Teams follow different ways of managing projects. | Reduced operational consistency and scalability. |

---

# 2. Improvement Opportunities

## Improvement 1 – Standardize Lead Management

**Current Problem**

Not every lead is entered into HubSpot.

**Recommendation**

Require every client inquiry, including referrals, to be logged in HubSpot before the discovery call.

**Expected Impact**

- Complete lead tracking
- Better sales visibility
- Improved reporting

---

## Improvement 2 – Standard Proposal Template

**Current Problem**

Each consultant prepares proposals differently.

**Recommendation**

Create a standardized proposal template with predefined sections and branding.

**Expected Impact**

- Consistent client experience
- Faster proposal creation
- Easier review process

---

## Improvement 3 – Mandatory Project Setup Checklist

**Current Problem**

Projects occasionally begin before Asana and shared folders are created.

**Recommendation**

Implement a project initiation checklist that must be completed before work begins.

**Expected Impact**

- Better documentation
- Improved project organization
- Reduced onboarding errors

---

## Improvement 4 – Centralized Documentation

**Current Problem**

Project documents are stored in multiple locations.

**Recommendation**

Store all project documentation in a single shared location linked to Asana.

**Expected Impact**

- Easier document retrieval
- Better collaboration
- Reduced duplication

---

## Improvement 5 – Standard Project Status Updates

**Current Problem**

Consultants provide updates using different communication channels.

**Recommendation**

Require all consultants to update project progress in Asana on a weekly basis.

**Expected Impact**

- Improved project visibility
- Better reporting
- Increased accountability

---

## Improvement 6 – Resource Planning

**Current Problem**

Resource allocation depends on spreadsheets and manual decisions.

**Recommendation**

Implement a centralized resource planning dashboard to track consultant availability and workload.

**Expected Impact**

- Balanced workloads
- Better utilization
- Reduced scheduling conflicts

---

## Improvement 7 – Billing Controls

**Current Problem**

Late timesheet submissions delay invoicing.

**Recommendation**

Introduce automated reminders and approval deadlines for time entry submissions.

**Expected Impact**

- Faster billing
- Improved cash flow
- Reduced manual follow-up
